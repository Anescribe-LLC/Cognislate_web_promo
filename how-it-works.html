How it works

Autofill where it helps.
Manual where it matters.

Cognislate prepares real consent PDFs with patient-specific details, then leaves the actual consent process manual: signatures, witness signatures, checkmarks, initials, cross-outs, and written instructions happen directly on the form.

Simple setup

Three steps, start to finish

1

Upload

Upload a PDF of your vetted consent form — check boxes, logos, and all.

2

Proof

Proof our AI-placed autofill fields. Anything that can be input digitally from an EMR — patient name, MRN, DOB, or date of signing. Critically, signature is not among these.

3

Scan

Scan a list or schedule — paper, or EMR on screen. Our AI lets you scan with your iPad and get to work with an electronically labeled but unsigned stack of consent forms. Happy consenting!
